# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Focus discussions on ideas, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or deliberate disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or ban contributors for violations.

## Reporting

To report misconduct, contact maintainers privately via the security channel in `SECURITY.md`.
